# ✅ brand/CHECKLIST — identity-system acceptance

## Strategy
- ☐ Positioning · audience · personality · differentiator stated; every visual choice traces to it.

## Logo & marks
- ☐ Primary lockup + symbol/wordmark; **clear space + min size + misuse list**; on-light/on-dark; favicon + OG.
- ☐ Supplied logos are the **real uploaded files** (no redraws).

## Color
- ☐ Palette as **Variables** (primary/secondary/accent/neutrals/semantic), each with role.
- ☐ **Every foreground/background pair states its contrast ratio and passes WCAG AA** (4.5:1 / 3:1 large).

## Type
- ☐ Type as real **text styles** with real specimens + a modular scale + web-font/fallback note.

## Voice & imagery & motion
- ☐ Voice principles + do/don't + **real example copy** per context (no lorem).
- ☐ Imagery/art direction with do/don't; motion spec card documented.

## Applications & deliverable
- ☐ Favicon · OG image · social · email · collateral built from the system.
- ☐ A shareable **Brand Guidelines** page assembles the rulebook.

## Craft & honesty (hard gates)
- ☐ Figma Sections · auto-layout · namespaced (`Brand/…`) · cover + page index · 8-pt rhythm.
- ☐ **No invented proof** anywhere; claims in copy are defensible; tokens trace to `BRAND-CONFIG`.
