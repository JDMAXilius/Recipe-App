## STEP 1 — PROPOSE the identity (→ log to `ALTAVIDA-BRAND-NOTES.md`)
- **Logo direction:** 2–3 options (wordmark/monogram); pick one, one-line rationale.
- **Palette:** primary/secondary/accent/neutrals/semantic — with **AA contrast pairs** proven.
- **Type:** a display/heading/body pairing + modular scale + web-font source.
- **Voice:** principles + do/don't + real example copy (headline · CTA · error).
- **Imagery / motion:** art direction + motion principles.
Write these into `altavida/BRAND-CONFIG.md` (marked `⟨proposed⟩`).

## STEP 2 — DEFINE the site (→ `altavida/SITE-MAP.md`)
Propose the marketing-site pages + each page's sections + one primary CTA + SEO, from the positioning
(default: Home · About · Services/Product · Contact · Legal). Real, on-brand draft copy — no lorem.
