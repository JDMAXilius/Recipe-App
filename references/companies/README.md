# References — company & competitor app studies

Deep-dive analyses of apps and companies worth learning from, written to inform **Otto** (the recipe +
meal-plan app) and the upcoming **Auto** website/ecosystem. Each study covers product, design system,
brand voice, pricing, and — most importantly — **concrete, mapped lessons** for our own work (plus
what *not* to blind-copy).

> These are research references, not specs. They capture what makes a product excellent and how that
> translates to Otto/Auto. Sources are cited at the bottom of each file.
